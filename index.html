Trust & Privacy

Your Data is Yours. Period.

We built this app for one reason: your safety. Our entire system is designed around your privacy. Here is our simple promise to you.

End-to-End Encryption

Your log data (names, photos, locations) is encrypted on your device *before* it's sent. We cannot see it.

The 48-Hour Timer

If your timer expires, our system *automatically* sends your encrypted log to emergency contacts or local authorities.

Permanent Deletion

The *moment* you safely end your meetup, your *entire* encrypted log is permanently deleted from our servers. We keep nothing.
